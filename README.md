2. Critical Manufacturing Steps
A. Tray Plate Sub-Assembly (MB_V07.1-2_Tray Plate Assembly)
Parallelism: The Side Strips (Left & Right) must be mounted exactly parallel to each other. Any deviation will cause the tray to bind when inserted into the U-Shell.
Fold Accuracy: The tray plate has a 267mm width between the mounting flanges. Ensure the bend deduction is calculated for a 1.2mm material thickness to maintain this clearance.
Support Rack: The Tray Support Rack must be flush-mounted to provide structural integrity for the heavy electronic components.
B. Main Door & Security (1.MB_V07.1-2_MAIN DOOR)
Stack-up Height: The combined height of the Front Door Top Plate and Bottom Plate must not exceed 365.6mm.
Theft Protection: The Theft Plate features specific cutouts that must align perfectly with the door frame to prevent prying points.
Hole Alignment: The Ø4.2mm and Ø5.0mm mounting holes must be checked for pitch accuracy to ensure the hinges and locking hardware align with the U-Shell frame.


3. Fabrication Callouts for the Shop Floor
Edge Treatment: All laser-cut edges on the Tray Plate and U-Shell must be machine-deburred. Sharp edges may damage internal wiring or pose a safety risk during assembly.
Bending: Maintain a standard R1.0 internal bend radius.
Surface Preparation: If powder coating is required, all M3 and M4 threaded holes must be masked.
Hardware: Ensure PEM nuts/Clinch nuts are installed perpendicular to the sheet surface to avoid cross-threading during final assembly.
